## Scoring Methodology

Our AI evaluates multiple dimensions to generate a comprehensive score:

### Overall Score (0-100)
- **90-100**: Excellent match - High interview probability
- **75-89**: Good match - Strong candidate
- **60-74**: Fair match - Some improvements needed
- **45-59**: Moderate match - Significant improvements recommended
- **0-44**: Poor match - Major revisions required

### Category Scores
- **Skills Match** (30%): Alignment of technical and soft skills
- **Experience Relevance** (30%): Job history and project alignment
- **Content Quality** (20%): Writing quality and presentation
- **Keyword Optimization** (20%): ATS-friendly keywords
