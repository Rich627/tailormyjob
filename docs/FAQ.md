## General Questions

### What is TailorMyJob?

TailorMyJob is an AI-powered platform that analyzes your resume against job descriptions to provide detailed feedback, matching scores, and actionable recommendations to improve your chances of landing interviews.

### How does it work?

1. Upload your resume (PDF, DOC, or DOCX)
2. Paste the job description you're applying for
3. Our AI analyzes the match between your resume and the job
4. Get detailed insights and recommendations in under 30 seconds

### Who is TailorMyJob for?

- **Job Seekers**: Looking to optimize resumes for specific positions
- **Career Coaches**: Helping clients improve their job application materials
- **HR Professionals**: Screening candidates more efficiently
- **Students**: Preparing for their first job applications

### What makes TailorMyJob different?

- **AI-Powered**: Uses Claude 3.5 Sonnet, one of the most advanced AI models
- **Fast**: Results in under 30 seconds
- **Detailed**: Comprehensive scoring and specific recommendations
- **Secure**: Enterprise-grade security with AWS infrastructure
- **Bilingual**: Full support for English and Traditional Chinese

## Results & Recommendations

### What does the score mean?

Scores range from 0-100:
- **90-100**: Excellent match
- **75-89**: Good match
- **60-74**: Fair match
- **45-59**: Moderate match
- **0-44**: Poor match

### How do I interpret the results?

Results include:
- **Overall Score**: General compatibility
- **Category Scores**: Skills, experience, education, etc.
- **Strengths**: What you're doing well
- **Improvements**: Areas to enhance
- **Missing Skills**: Skills mentioned in job description but not in resume

### Are the recommendations guaranteed to work?

Recommendations are suggestions based on AI analysis. Results depend on many factors including the job market, competition, and how you implement changes.

### Should I apply if my score is low?

A score is just one factor. If you're passionate about the role and meet minimum requirements, consider applying anyway. Use our recommendations to improve your application.

### Can I get help implementing recommendations?

Yes! Pro and Enterprise plans include:
- Detailed implementation guides
- Email support
- Live chat support (Enterprise)
