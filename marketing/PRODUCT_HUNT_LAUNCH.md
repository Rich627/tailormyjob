## Launch Day Checklist

### Before Launch (1 week)
- [ ] All media assets ready
- [ ] Landing page optimized for PH traffic
- [ ] Social media posts scheduled
- [ ] Email to existing users drafted
- [ ] Friends/supporters notified

### Launch Day
- [ ] Post at 12:01 AM PST (best time)
- [ ] First comment posted immediately
- [ ] Share on Twitter
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn
- [ ] Share in relevant communities
- [ ] Respond to all comments within 1 hour

### After Launch
- [ ] Thank all supporters
- [ ] Follow up with interested users
- [ ] Document lessons learned
